How the rig works

Three moves, one ranking that's actually yours.

DRAG

Set your weights

Seven sliders — ROM, battery, price, storage, charging, display, camera. Push what you care about up, pull the rest down. The math updates as you move.

CUT

Set deal-breakers

Some specs aren't a preference, they're a wall. Flip a toggle to hard-exclude any phone under your battery, charging, or display floor — no amount of weighting saves it.

Every rank card breaks down its score by category, and hovering shows the plain-language reason a phone landed where it did. No black box.
